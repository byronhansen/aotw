Kanye West - Graduation

8.8

easter egg!

After touring with U2 in 2005 and 2006, Kanye got his first taste of the arena and made it his goal to make 'stadium music.' Gradution was that album. With perfect highs, booming lows, and steller stereo vocals, Graduation is meant to blasted on the biggest speakers you own. A must listen.
