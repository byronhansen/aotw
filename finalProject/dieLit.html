Playboi Carti - Die Lit

8.6

easter egg!

Three hours before Die Lit was released, Carti tweeted this. In classic Carti style, this single tweet was the only promotion for the album. Die Lit peaked at #3 on the Billboard 200 and remained on the list for 47 consecutive weeks. Compared to his debut self-titled mixtape, Die Lit is more refined, a manifestation of Carti's evolution and his realization of his potential. An extremely fun and hyper listen.
