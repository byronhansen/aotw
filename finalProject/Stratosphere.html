Duster - Stratosphere

8.6

easter egg!

As Pusha T is to the rap scene, Duster is your favorite indie band's favorite indie band. Hailing from San Jose, California, Duster has garnered a legendary underground status within the indie community, rarely and injustly mentioned in modern and mainstream discussions of the 90s indie, soft-pop scene. With standout tracks such as Gold Dust and Topical Solution, Stratosphere is perfect and flawed, as an indie album should be. A great listen.
